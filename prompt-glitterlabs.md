# Glitter Technology Ventures Pvt. Ltd. Website — Original Build Prompt

> This document captures the original product brief used to generate the Glitter Technology Ventures Pvt. Ltd. static website.

---

## Company Overview

The company is an **AI Studio, Generative AI Content and Workforce Distribution platform** focused on delivering **outcome-driven AI solutions** across:

- Edge AI (real-time, on-device intelligence)
- Vertical AI (domain-specific AI systems)
- AI Agents (autonomous workflows and copilots)
- LLM Frameworks (tooling, orchestration, and pipelines)
- Generative AI Content (text, video, and image distribution at scale)

**Positioning:**
> "From AI experimentation → to real-world outcomes at scale"

---

## Website Goals

- Communicate **clarity, credibility, and technical depth**
- Showcase **real-world impact (outcomes > features)**
- Drive **inbound leads, hiring, and partnerships**
- Be optimized for **GitHub Pages (static hosting)**
- Make sure the site remains visually clean and easy to read.
---

## Design Requirements

- Vibrant, modern, premium UI
- Use a vibrant but light color palette: pastel backgrounds, light-themed sections, high-contrast text, and avoid dark or heavy
colors.
- Light theme with gradient accents (purple, blue, neon green)
- Glassmorphism + soft shadows
- Smooth animations (hover, scroll transitions)
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Use Tailwind CSS (CDN)

---

## Pages to Build

### 1. Home Page (`index.html`)

#### Banner / Hero Section
- Full-width visually rich banner
- Gradient animated background
- Headline: `"AI Systems That Deliver Outcomes — Not Just Demos"`
- Subheadline explaining AI Studio + workforce distribution positioning
- CTA buttons:
  - "Explore Solutions"
  - "Partner With Us"

#### Solutions Section

Break into 4 pillars:
- Edge AI
- Vertical AI
- AI Agents
- LLM Frameworks

Each section must include:
- Problem → Solution → Outcome

#### Outcomes / Use Cases

Highlight measurable impact:
- "Reduced manual workflows by 70%"
- "Scaled content generation 10x"
- "Enabled real-time decisioning"

Focus on **business results and workforce productivity gains**

#### Content & Workforce Distribution Engine

Explain:
- AI-generated blogs, videos, and media
- Workforce orchestration (human + AI agents)
- Multi-channel distribution pipelines
- Scalable execution systems

#### CTA Section
- "Start Building with AI"
- "Work With Us"

---

### 2. About Page (`about.html`)

- Mission, vision, and philosophy
- "Why we exist" narrative
- Emphasize: AI + workforce transformation + content at scale
- Focus on builders, founders, and enterprises

---

### 3. Careers Page (`careers.html`)

- CTA: "Apply Now"

Deep dive into:
